Pepperoni Pizza Recipe

Pepperoni Pizza

Description

This recipe will teach you have to make a delicious pepperoni pizza! The contents of the pizza will be, mozzarella cheese, pepperoni of course, garlic powder, onion powder, dried basil, oregano, and tomato paste.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 can of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil, crushed
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • sugar
  • salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 3 1/4 all-purpose flour
  • 2 envelopes FLEISCHMANN'S pizza crust yeast
  • 1 1/3 cups of very warm water
  • 1/3 cup oil
  • 6 ounces of pepperoni
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Combine all sauce ingredients with 1/2 cup water in a medium bowl; set aside for flavours to develop while making crust.
  2. Combine 2 cups of flour with the dry yeast, 1 tablespoon sugar and 1 1/2 teapsoons salt. Add water and oil, and mix until well blendeed (about 1 minute). Gradually add enough remaining flour slowly, until a soft, sticky dough ball is formed
  3. Knead for about 4 minutes, on a floured surface, until dough is smooth and elastic. Add more flour, if needed.
  4. Divide dough in half. Pat each half (with floured hands) into a 12-inch greased pizza pan OR roll dough to fit pans.
  5. Bake for 18 to 20 minutes until crusts are browned and cheese is bubbly. For best results, rotate pizza pans between top and bottom oven racks halfway through baking.
